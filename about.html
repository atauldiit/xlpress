Reaching for the peaks of success

“A story of a million words starts with a single letter and invariably has a lot of punctuation, proof reading and revisions!”

Founded in 2011, xlpress was formed to fill an emerging gap in the market. With a strong reputation for printing excellence we soon became one of the leading print firms in East Anglia.

Printed materials are still an essential part of many businesses. At xlpress we strive to provide you with high quality print solutions to ensure all of your printing requirements are met.

Our unrivalled expertise in file preparation, print quality, specialist print finishes coupled with our extensive industry knowledge and network of trade professionals make us the perfect print partner for any established or growing company.

Our continual investment in print technology, software solutions and highly skilled staff ensures we have the tools, technology and team to complete even the most complex projects.
