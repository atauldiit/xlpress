ABOUT US

Founded in 2011, xlpress was formed to fill an emerging gap in the market. With a strong reputation for printing excellence we soon became one of the leading print firms in East Anglia.

WHAT WE DO

  • We run a 24 hour, 5 days a week print plant, ensuring that even the most critical jobs are not missed - if we have to do an extra shift, so be it!
  • We allocate dedicated account handlers to each client, ensuring that every client is looked after and given the right care and attention.
  • We have a fully staffed and equipped print plant offering expert advice, guidance, support and correction from enquiry to delivery.
