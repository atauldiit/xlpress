It isn’t always black and white

What you see on your computer screen may not be exactly what get’s printed. This is where Pre-Press comes into it’s own. This essential stage at the start of the printing process helps to identify and address any issues with the artwork before it even gets to print and is vital when working to tight deadlines or jobs that are colour critical. Working through a list of “pre-flight” checks our pre-press team can spot items such as low resolution images, incorrect colour setup, misalignment

or the exclusion of bleed to name but a few. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail and close working partnerships with our clients in resolving any artwork issues, checking every single job that comes in and giving each job it’s due care.
